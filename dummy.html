Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Pro2Buddies is a premium web development agency founded by two friends, Sibidass (Lead Developer) and Maheswaran (Creative Designer). We combine technical expertise with creative design to deliver websites that not only look stunning but also drive real business results. Based in Chennai, India, we serve clients globally.

Our standard landing pages are delivered within 3 days, while multi-page business websites typically take 7 days from kickoff to launch. Complex e-commerce or custom web applications may require 2-4 weeks depending on features. We always provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

We specialize in modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React, Node.js, Python, and SQL databases. For styling, we use Tailwind CSS and custom CSS animations. We're also experienced with various CMS platforms like WordPress and headless CMS solutions. Our tech stack is chosen based on your specific project requirements for optimal performance.
